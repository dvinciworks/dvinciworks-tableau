]]> BBC Sport have just published their Price of Football survey where they looked at the cost of tickets,

The following visualisation compares the BBC headline cost ‘Cheapest Day Out’ (the total cost of the
cheapest ticket, a programme, a pie and a cup of tea) to both goals scored and points won last season. 
When looking at the Value For Money viz then the further above the trend line a club appears then the
better value it gives for your money.  Obviously the furthest a club appears below the line then the
worst value for money it provides.  Crawley Town and the Peterborough are the stars while West Ham are
not…

]]> With the recent increases in VAT and fuel excise duty the cost of petrol and diesel in the UK has hit record highs. The Office for National Statistics complies and publishes weekly averages for diesel and petrol. Here is my visual representation made using their data. The upper viz charts the weekly fuel prices over time.

The lower viz allows the user to select an Indexation Date and then charts the % difference in price from that date.

The background shading of both shows the changing VAT rate.

Powered by Tableau

The % Difference in Price viz was created quite quickly and only required the creation of a parameter, two calculated fields and a few reference lines.

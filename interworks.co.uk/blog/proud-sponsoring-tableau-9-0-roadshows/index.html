Proud to be Sponsoring The Tableau 9.0 Roadshow…

Posted by Victoria Ponsford on 24 March, 2015  

Register for an upcoming roadshow here.

Since the release of Beta 9.0, the InterWorks team have been getting excited over the numerous new and enhanced features the upgrade brings. We’ve written blogs, held webinars and have eagerly been awaiting its public launch.

We’re getting ever closer to launch date; and in celebration, we’ll be sponsoring the Tableau Roadshows across Europe, where you’ll get a chance to see 9.0 in action. Join us at a location near you; where you’re invited to see all of the amazing new features and capabilities of Tableau 9.0, network with fellow Tableau enthusiasts and most importantly, say “Hi!”

There were almost too many new features to choose from but here are the team’s “Top Ten”.

1. New Userface Enhancements – read our blog here.
2. Data Interpreter and Clean-Up Tools (e.g. pivot and split) – click here.
3. Drag & Drop Analytics.
4. Improved Performance – read our blog here.
5. Responsive tool-tips – read our blog here.
6. Level of Detail Calculations – check out our vlog here.
7. Formatting for Story Points
8. New Data Connections – read our blog here.
9. Creating Calculated Fields – read our blog here.
10. Colour Picker – easily embed your company’s colours into a view!

Why would it benefit you?

Quite simply, because you’ll be able to see Tableau 9.0 in action before anyone else! You’ll have first-hand access to hands on-demos of all the new features, and you can get involved in a wide choice of breakout sessions, led by Tableau themselves. New to Tableau, or a veteran – these events cater for everyone. Not only will you get to hear from a keynote speaker, you’ll get to network with fellow enthusiasts and share best practise tips and tricks. Oh, and of course there’s the obligatory happy hour to look forward to! It’s the perfect chance to learn everything about Tableau 9.0. Best of all, each event is completely free.

See Power Tools in Action!

The launch of Tableau 9.0 is exciting enough, but… InterWorks has something extra to show all event attendees – our revolutionary Power Tools for Tableau. People are already being delighted about this suite of essential Tableau tools. We’ll be giving live demos of Power Tools at each event, so be sure to stop by and see what they’re all about. For more info, click here.

When and Where?

The Roadshows begin on 16th April in Paris, France. Please see below for the list of dates and locations, or our events page for more info:

16th April 2015 @ 13:00 – Paris – The Four Seasons Hotel

23rd April 2015 @ 13:00 – Amsterdam – Novotel Amsterdam City Hotel

30th April 2015 @ 13:00 – London – Grange, St Paul’s Hotel

7th May 2015 @ 13:00 – Munich – Sofitel Munich Bayerpost Hotel

We hope you’ll be able to make it to one of these events. If you do, make sure to pop by our stand and say Hi! Register by clicking here.

Here is another approach to your formula for creating the spark values:

PREVIOUS_VALUE(“”)+STR(ZN(ROUND((SUM([Sales])/WINDOW_MAX(SUM([Sales])))*9)))

This will do what all your lookup functions do, concatenating a spark value for each month. The issue you describe of missing months in the data can be resolved with worksheet layout, partitioning and a helper calcs for filtering the display and/or repeating this calculated value for every mark.

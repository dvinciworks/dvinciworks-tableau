]]> By: jL http://interworks.co.uk/blog/linear-regression-trend-lines/#comment-63514 Wed, 04 Jun 2014 09:50:16 +0000 http://interworks.co.uk/?p=10093#comment-63514 Thank you very much for this post, I have been trying to work with trend line parameters for a long time, so your example was very useful (it’s too bad that Tableau doesn’t let us access them as calculated field).

Because I was working with graphs over time and not X Y graphs, I had to adjust your formulae and thought it could be useful for later post-readers.
Instead of WINDOW_SUM(SUM(x)), use :

WINDOW_SUM(ATTR(FLOAT([my_date_dimension]))) if you are displaying [my_date_dimension]
or
WINDOW_SUM(ATTR(MONTH([my_date_dimension]))) if you are displaying Month Of [my_date_dimension] for instance.

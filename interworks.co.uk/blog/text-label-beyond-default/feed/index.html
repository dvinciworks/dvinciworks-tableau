Yes, you can get the currency symbol.
What you need to do is get the Format Numbers panel on the left-hand side panel. Best done by right-clicking on what you want to change and select Format…

Then, in the Numbers panel, select “Number (custom)”. In that option, you can speciffy a prefix (or suffix), so just add your currency symbol as a prefix.

good luck!

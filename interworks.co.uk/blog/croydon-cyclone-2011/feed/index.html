There are a few ways to address this issue, my preferred is to use a single measure pill with a formatted axis, instead of two measure pills with one revered. When you have two measure pills like you do currently, the only option is a static axis, but that generates a lot of unneeded white space.

On this view and some of the others, the lack of an axis seems to make it more difficult for me to parse the information. For example on the “Holes and pars” sheet, I have to guess is the axis % of total for the division or the count.

Thankfully this is on Tableau Public, and I am able to remix it and create views that enable me to answer my own questions. Thank you for this Andy.

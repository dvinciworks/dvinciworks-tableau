There are no changes in permissions in Tableau 9, and permissions don’t change as part of the upgrade from 8 to 9.
Is this change affecting just one person or everyone?
If it is just one person, I would take a look at the person’s permissions and ensure that they have Web Editing rights for that project.
If it is everyone, I would check the Server settings to ensure that Web Authoring is enabled for that site. (This is under settings)

Careers at InterWorks

Business Intelligence Consultant

Your job is to:

  • Help our clients see and understand their data
  • Provide clients with meaningful and actionable insight to their data
  • Enable clients to drive better value from all of their resources
 

You will do this by:

  • Providing business intelligence consulting skills to a wide range of organisations
  • Creating, delivering and supporting dashboards that exemplify industry best-practice
  • Possessing a high level of competence in all aspects of data visualisation including certification in Tableau
  • Possessing a high level of competence in certain core programming and/or database languages such as SQL, ,Net, C#, Javascript, Python or others as required
  • Communicating clearly with others by email, phone and in person
  • Participating in sales and marketing activities such as seminars, trade shows, webinars
  • Sharing your knowledge with others through training courses, blogs, demo sessions
  • Accurately recording and documenting interactions with customers, project notes, support issues, etc
 

Your skills:

  • Analytical (of course!) – you have an inquisitive mind with a passion for technology but not for its own sake
  • Passionate – you really understand Tableau and the power of data visualisation
  • Accurate – you’re reliable, methodical, practical and organised, even under pressure
  • Communications – you are equally adept at communicating the usefulness and power of analytics to both technical and non-technical audiences. You take the time to understand your audience and remain confident in important situations
  • A domain expert – you have a proven ability with the core programming languages mentioned above
  • Commercial skills – you have a good grounding in the fundamentals of commerce and finance. You have both the enthusiasm and the ability to show how analytics can help any organisation deliver excellence
  • Have some experience of consulting or working face to face with clients
 

Does this sound like you?

Account Manager - London

Department:  Sales
Location:  Within travelling distance of Bournemouth and London

 

What you’ll be doing …

As an Account Manager, you’ll be responsible for driving sales of Tableau products and our professional services in the United Kingdom, exceeding sales objectives.  You will manage all aspects of the sales process including lead generation, lead management, qualification, evaluation, closing new business and account care. This role is a quota-carrying sales position that plays an integral part in the success of the company and involves some UK travel.

 

Who you are …

  • Domain:  Your primary skill is an experience with and passion for business intelligence, analytics, data or databases.  A technical or business background is preferred.
  • Experience:  Some prior experience in sales is preferred but not essential. Your ability to sell flows from your passion for analytics and the insight that data can provide.
  • Performer:  You love to achieve and you’re hungry for success.
  • Amazing Salesperson:  You have a desire, even an obsession, to bring new customers to InterWorks and maximise revenue through prospecting, qualifying, managing and closing sales opportunities. You are a closer.
  • Excellent Communication:  You practise excellent, effective communication with management, customers and other team members. You know what to say and more importantly, how to say it.
  • Missionary:  Highly driven individual with an execution focus, a strong sense of urgency and a belief in our mission. You go beyond relationship management.
  • Go-Getter:  Willing to go the extra mile with a strong work ethic; self-directed and resourceful.
  • Cross Cultural Experience:  You have experience relating to customers from a variety of cultural backgrounds.
  • Team Player: Participate in team-building and company-growth activities including strategy setting, sales training, marketing efforts and customer care.
  • Future:  You will become an indispensable member of our sales team. As the company grows  there is the possibility of a position as a sales manager.
 

Does this sound like you?

Company Description

InterWorks is a globally recognised leader of Business Intelligence services and consulting. Our greatest strengths come from our confidence with leading BI software, our extensive knowledge of IT optimisation, our highly engaged network of employees, and providing the most complete BI solutions to our clients. Our chosen BI tool of choice is Tableau Software. Tableau makes fast analytics and visualization products that help people see and understand data of any size or format.

]]> Every day last week the Guardian Datablog revealed the top 25 films from a different genre in the new Guardian/Observer greatest films of all time list. The Datablog then asked for help in visualising the information and below you will find the visualisation I came up with using Tableau Public.

The visualisation can be filtered to view specific genres using the drop down list whilst by clicking on the graphs themselves you can update all of the other graphs. Using these interactive filters you can find out who the top directors and actors were in each decade and even filter through to see their films in the tabular data. By clicking on the name of the films I have also included a direct link through to that film’s page on the Internet Movie Database.
